Location & Directions

Campus Location

Imperial College of Nursing is located on Main University Road in Kabal, Swat. The campus is easily accessible from major areas of Swat Valley.

From Mingora City:
  • Take University Road towards Kabal
  • Continue straight for approximately 8 km
  • College is on the right side after Kabal Chowk
  • Travel time: 15-20 minutes
Public Transport:
  • Local buses available from Mingora to Kabal
  • College bus service for students
  • Taxi services available throughout Swat

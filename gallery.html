Flood Relief Activity 2022

Students and faculty providing healthcare support to flood-affected communities

In response to the devastating floods in Swat Valley, our students and faculty organized a comprehensive relief effort providing medical care, hygiene kits, and essential supplies to affected communities.

Activities Included:

  • Medical camps in flood-affected areas
  • Distribution of hygiene and first aid kits
  • Health education sessions
  • Psychological support for affected families
  • Collaboration with local health authorities

Welcome Party for F25 Batch

Warm welcome to new nursing students

A grand welcome ceremony was organized for the Freshman 2025 batch, introducing new students to campus life, faculty members, and senior students. The event aimed to make new students feel at home in their nursing journey.

Event Activities:

  • Orientation sessions by faculty
  • Campus tour and facility introduction
  • Ice-breaking activities and games
  • Meet and greet with senior students
  • Cultural performances
  • Welcome dinner

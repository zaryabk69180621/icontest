Biomedical Sciences Laboratory

Integrating basic sciences with clinical nursing practice

Our Science Laboratory provides nursing students with hands-on experience in fundamental biomedical sciences essential for evidence-based nursing practice. The lab bridges theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students for clinical decision-making and patient care.

Laboratory Divisions

Anatomy & Physiology

Study of human body structure and function through models, charts, and specimens.

  • Human skeleton models
  • Organ system charts
  • Histology slides
  • Muscle manikins
  • Physiology experiments

Microbiology

Study of microorganisms, infection control, and sterile techniques.

  • Microscope workstations
  • Culture media preparation
  • Sterilization equipment
  • Pathogen identification
  • Antibiotic sensitivity

Biochemistry

Basic biochemical analysis relevant to nursing practice and diagnostics.

  • Urinalysis equipment
  • Blood glucose testing
  • pH measurement
  • Enzyme studies
  • Metabolism experiments

Practical Sessions

Session Schedule

  • Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
  • Batch size: 20 students maximum
  • Advance booking required

Session Requirements

  • Lab coat mandatory
  • Closed-toe shoes required
  • Safety goggles provided
  • Pre-lab reading completion

Safety Protocols

Hand Hygiene

Mandatory hand washing before and after sessions

Protective Gear

Lab coats, gloves, and goggles must be worn

Hazard Awareness

Chemical and biological hazard training

Emergency Response

First aid and spill management procedures

Prohibited Activities

No food or drinks No unauthorized experiments No mobile phone use No specimen removal

Anatomy Museum & Laboratory

Comprehensive study of human anatomy through specimens, models, and digital resources

Human Anatomy Learning Center

Integrating traditional anatomical study with modern educational technology

Our Anatomy Museum provides nursing students with an immersive learning environment featuring over 200 anatomical specimens, detailed models, and state-of-the-art digital resources. The facility supports comprehensive study of human structure essential for clinical practice and surgical understanding.

Anatomy Collections

Osteology Collection

Complete skeletons and individual bones for detailed study of the human skeletal system.

  • Articulated skeletons
  • Disarticulated bone sets
  • Comparative anatomy specimens
  • Pathological bone specimens
  • Development stages collection

Organ Systems

Prosection specimens and models of all major organ systems for system-based learning.

  • Cardiovascular system specimens
  • Respiratory system models
  • Digestive tract prosections
  • Neurological specimens
  • Urinary system models

Neuroanatomy

Specialized collection for studying the nervous system, brain, and spinal cord anatomy.

  • Whole brain specimens
  • Brain section models
  • Spinal cord cross-sections
  • Cranial nerve demonstrations
  • Neurovascular specimens

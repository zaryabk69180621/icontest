Leadership Messages

Words of wisdom and vision from our leadership team

Mr. Khalid

Principal

BSN, MSN (KMU)

Principal's Message

Welcome to Imperial College of Nursing Kabal Swat, a premier institution dedicated to excellence in nursing education. As Principal, I am honored to lead an institution that is committed to shaping the future of healthcare in Swat Valley and beyond.

Our college was established with a vision to produce competent, compassionate, and ethical nursing professionals who can meet the evolving healthcare needs of our society. We believe that nursing is not just a profession, but a calling that requires dedication, empathy, and continuous learning.

At Imperial College of Nursing, we provide a nurturing environment where students can develop their clinical skills, critical thinking abilities, and professional values. Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and strong clinical partnerships ensure that our graduates are well-prepared to excel in their careers and make meaningful contributions to healthcare.

I invite you to join us on this journey of learning and growth. Together, we can build a healthier future for our communities and make a positive impact on the lives of those we serve.

Mr. Shawkat Zarin

Director

MPH, Post RN BScN

Director's Message

As the Director of Imperial College of Nursing, I am proud to welcome you to our institution. We are committed to providing quality nursing education that combines theoretical knowledge with extensive clinical practice.

Our curriculum is designed to meet the standards set by the Pakistan Nursing Council and Khyber Medical University, ensuring that our graduates are equipped with the knowledge and skills required for professional nursing practice. We focus on developing competent healthcare providers who can deliver patient-centered care in various clinical settings.

What sets us apart is our emphasis on practical training. Our students have access to modern simulation labs and clinical placements in reputed hospitals, where they gain hands-on experience under the supervision of experienced nursing professionals.

I encourage prospective students to explore our programs and discover how Imperial College of Nursing can help you achieve your career goals in the noble field of nursing.

Prof. Raza Shah

BOG Member

PHD Bio Chemistry

BOG Member's Message

On behalf of the Board of Governors, I extend a warm welcome to Imperial College of Nursing Kabal Swat. Our institution represents a commitment to excellence in healthcare education and a vision for improving health outcomes in the Swat Valley region.

The establishment of this college was driven by the need for well-trained nursing professionals in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. We recognized that quality nursing education is essential for building a robust healthcare system that can serve the needs of our growing population.

Our commitment extends beyond academic excellence. We aim to instill in our students a sense of social responsibility and a dedication to serving underserved communities. The Swat Valley, with its unique healthcare challenges, provides an ideal setting for our students to develop their skills while making a real difference in people's lives.

I am confident that our graduates will become leaders in nursing practice, education, and research, contributing to the advancement of healthcare not only in Pakistan but also internationally.

Vision for the Future

We envision Imperial College of Nursing as a center of excellence that:

  • Produces world-class nursing professionals
  • Contributes to nursing research and innovation
  • Partners with healthcare institutions for community service
  • Sets standards for nursing education in the region

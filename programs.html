Generic BSc Nursing (GBSN)

4-Year Undergraduate Program

Program Overview

The Generic Bachelor of Science in Nursing (GBSN) is a comprehensive 4-year program designed to prepare students for professional nursing practice. The program combines theoretical knowledge with extensive clinical experience to develop competent, compassionate, and ethical nursing professionals.

Key Features

  • 4-year comprehensive nursing program
  • KMU affiliated and PNC approved curriculum
  • Extensive clinical rotations in affiliated hospitals
  • Modern simulation labs for hands-on training
  • Qualified and experienced faculty
  • Career guidance and placement support

Skills Laboratory

Advanced simulation training for nursing excellence

Advanced Simulation Center

State-of-the-art facilities for hands-on nursing training

Our Skills Laboratory is a cutting-edge simulation center designed to provide students with realistic clinical training in a safe, controlled environment. Equipped with the latest medical technology and simulation equipment, it serves as the primary training ground for developing essential nursing skills.

Realistic simulation scenarios

Modern medical equipment

Expert supervision and feedback

Equipment & Facilities

Simulation Manikins

High-fidelity patient simulators that mimic human physiological responses, allowing students to practice complex clinical scenarios.

  • Adult patient simulators
  • Pediatric manikins
  • Childbirth simulators
  • CPR training dummies

Clinical Equipment

Complete range of medical equipment for practicing clinical procedures and nursing interventions.

  • IV training arms
  • Injection pads
  • Wound care kits
  • Catheterization sets
  • Bed-making equipment

Monitoring Systems

Advanced patient monitoring equipment for practicing vital signs assessment and interpretation.

  • Digital blood pressure monitors
  • Pulse oximeters
  • ECG machines
  • Temperature monitoring devices
  • Glucometers

Training Programs

Basic Nursing Skills

Fundamental nursing procedures including vital signs monitoring, medication administration, wound care, and basic patient care techniques.

Semester 1-2 Mandatory

Advanced Clinical Procedures

Complex nursing interventions including IV therapy, catheterization, NG tube insertion, and emergency response procedures.

Semester 3-4 Advanced Level

Specialized Simulation

Scenario-based training in specialized areas including obstetric care, pediatric nursing, psychiatric interventions, and critical care.

Semester 5-8 Specialized

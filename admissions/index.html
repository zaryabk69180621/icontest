Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common admission queries

The minimum eligibility criteria include:

  • FSC (Pre-Medical) with minimum 50% marks from a recognized board
  • Appearance in Khyber Medical University (KMU) Common Admission Test (CAT)
  • Successful appearance in college admission interview

To apply for KMU CAT:

  1. Visit the official Khyber Medical University website
  2. Register for the Common Admission Test during the announced admission period
  3. Pay the required test fee through designated banks
  4. Download your admit card from the portal
  5. Appear for the test at the designated center

Required documents include:

  • FSC (Pre-Medical) Marksheet & Certificate (original + 3 attested copies)
  • Matriculation Marksheet & Certificate (original + 3 attested copies)
  • KMU CAT Score Card (original + 2 copies)
  • Domicile Certificate (original + 3 copies)
  • CNIC/B-Form (original + 3 copies)
  • Character Certificate from last institution
  • 6 recent passport-size photographs
